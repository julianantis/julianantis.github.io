Dreams of Yore

Florence · March 2026

Rest will follow.

Initiation
“Now you are free”
Worn and beaten is your path
But trodden not an aeon:
Enter wary here our wrath
Perpetuates your paean.

Witness of your due return
Is squealing in a chasm:
Be forsaken once you yearn
And mark your lot phantasm.

Mortal steps are here illicit.
The crest takes no record.
Lethe sees attempted visit,
Denies you rued and sored.

Long and lonely and uncertain
Seems voyage at begin.
Will you fare beyond the curtain?
The end lies far within.
